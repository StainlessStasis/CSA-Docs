# Support & Feedback
### Where can I go to receive support?
I do not have my own Discord server at the moment, but I do have a [support channel](https://discord.com/channels/934267676354834442/1387875174120165496)
in the [Cobblemon](https://discord.com/invite/cobblemon) server. In `content-zone-help`, search for Cobblemon Spawn Alerts.
Feel free to ping me - I don't mind, just don't spam it. My username is `stasis_the_shattered`.

:::warning READ THIS PLEASE
**DO NOT** send a friend request or DM me for support, unless I am otherwise entirely unreachable.
I will ignore your request. Keep it to the support channel, so that others can look back on the conversation 
and use it to solve their own issues.
:::

### Where can I give feedback/suggestions?
I've set up a Google forms [survey](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSek3U1Df_Ycwb_h5R1DPQKedsTzK9eTD2jPayqD3zw7oAJXkg/viewform) 
which just has a couple short questions. Should take 5 minutes max, and all responses are optional.

If you wish to give more detailed feedback, or have anything to say that isn't covered in the survey (e.g. feature suggestions), 
send it in Discord. See the above for how to contact me.
