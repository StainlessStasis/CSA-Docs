### Dependencies
:::info
[Ember's Text API](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/embers-text-api): Dependency for CSA in versions 1.12.0+.
Lets you do almost anything with text, including custom presets, effects, and even animations. Very cool mod indeed. <br />
[Adventure](https://docs.papermc.io/adventure/): Bundled with CSA in versions 1.11.5 and below.
Contains [MiniMessage](https://docs.papermc.io/adventure/minimessage/), another text library. You'll need 
reference their formatting if you aren't on 1.12.0+.
:::

### Recommended
:::info
[Journeymap](https://modrinth.com/mod/journeymap): Fully integrated with CSA, allowing for automatic creation and deletion of waypoints.
[Cobblemon Spawn Notification](https://modrinth.com/mod/cobblemon-spawn-notification): The more well-known spawn notification mod -
more stable, works with minimal setup. Consider using it on its own, or even in conjunction with CSA.
[Cobblemon Pokenav](https://modrinth.com/mod/cobblemon-pokenav): It's just a good mod tbh. Does the same sort of thing
of helping you find Pokemon, y`know?
:::
